Mensuration Formulas

Mensuration involves calculating the area, volume, and surface area of various geometric shapes. Here are the essential formulas for different shapes.

1. Area Formulas

Rectangle

The area of a rectangle is given by:

Area of a Rectangle:
A = length × width

Square

The area of a square is given by:

Area of a Square:
A = side²

Triangle

The area of a triangle is given by:

Area of a Triangle:
A = 1/2 × base × height

Circle

The area of a circle is given by:

Area of a Circle:
A = πr²

2. Perimeter Formulas

Rectangle

The perimeter of a rectangle is given by:

Perimeter of a Rectangle:
P = 2 × (length + width)

Square

The perimeter of a square is given by:

Perimeter of a Square:
P = 4 × side

Triangle

The perimeter of a triangle is given by:

Perimeter of a Triangle:
P = a + b + c

Circle

The circumference (perimeter) of a circle is given by:

Circumference of a Circle:
C = 2πr

3. Surface Area Formulas

Cube

The surface area of a cube is given by:

Surface Area of a Cube:
SA = 6 × side²

Rectangular Prism (Cuboid)

The surface area of a rectangular prism is given by:

Surface Area of a Rectangular Prism:
SA = 2 × (length × width + width × height + height × length)

Cylinder

The surface area of a cylinder is given by:

Surface Area of a Cylinder:
SA = 2πr(h + r)

Sphere

The surface area of a sphere is given by:

Surface Area of a Sphere:
SA = 4πr²

4. Volume Formulas

Cube

The volume of a cube is given by:

Volume of a Cube:
V = side³

Rectangular Prism (Cuboid)

The volume of a rectangular prism is given by:

Volume of a Rectangular Prism:
V = length × width × height

Cylinder

The volume of a cylinder is given by:

Volume of a Cylinder:
V = πr²h

Sphere

The volume of a sphere is given by:

Volume of a Sphere:
V = 4/3 × πr³

5. Key Applications

  • Architecture: Designing and calculating the space and material required for buildings.
  • Engineering: Measuring and optimizing designs for machines and structures.
  • Interior Design: Planning and calculating materials needed for spaces and furniture.
