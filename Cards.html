Bootstrap Cards


Introduction to Bootstrap Cards

Bootstrap cards are a flexible and extensible content container with multiple variants and options. Cards can include images, text, and actions, making them a great way to display information.

Basic Card Example

<div class="card">
    <div class="card-body">
        <h5 class="card-title">Card Title</h5>
        <p class="card-text">Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.</p>
        <a href="#" class="btn btn-primary">Go somewhere</a>
    </div>
</div>

Expected Output:

Card Title

Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.

Go somewhere

Card with Image

<div class="card">
    <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/150" class="card-img-top" alt="... ">
    <div class="card-body">
        <h5 class="card-title">Card Title</h5>
        <p class="card-text">This is a card with an image on top.</p>
    </div>
</div>

Expected Output:

Card Title

This is a card with an image on top.

Card Grouping

You can create groups of cards for better presentation:

<div class="card-group">
    <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
            <h5 class="card-title">Card 1</h5>
            <p class="card-text">This is card number 1.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
            <h5 class="card-title">Card 2</h5>
            <p class="card-text">This is card number 2.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
            <h5 class="card-title">Card 3</h5>
            <p class="card-text">This is card number 3.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

Expected Output:

Card 1

This is card number 1.

Card 2

This is card number 2.

Card 3

This is card number 3.

Summary

Bootstrap cards are a versatile component that can be used to present a variety of content in a structured manner. They can include images, text, buttons, and even group multiple cards together.
